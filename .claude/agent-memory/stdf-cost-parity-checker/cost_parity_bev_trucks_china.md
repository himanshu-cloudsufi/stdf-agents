BEV 49t heavy tractors achieved TCO service-level cost parity with LNG tractors in **2019–2020** in China (normalized LNG fuel CNY 4.3/kg basis). As of 2024, BEV TCO is 1.319 CNY/km vs. LNG 1.806 CNY/km — a 27.0% BEV advantage. Condition status: **MET**.

Purchase-price parity crossover is model-derived at 2024.8 (late 2024 / early 2025). 2024 observed prices: BEV CNY 460,000 vs. LNG CNY 420,000 (9.5% BEV premium, inside 10% threshold).

TCO inflection (>15% BEV advantage) crossed in 2021–2022.

**Why:** Ground-transport disruption where LFP battery cost-curve dynamics (16.70%/yr learning rate) are the primary driver of incumbent displacement.

**How to apply:** When this disruption is referenced in future runs, the cost parity determination is MET as of analysis date 2026-03-20. The 3-point CNY tractor price series (R-squared = 0.993, spuriously high) introduces ±1 yr uncertainty on the purchase-price parity year but does not affect the TCO MET determination.
