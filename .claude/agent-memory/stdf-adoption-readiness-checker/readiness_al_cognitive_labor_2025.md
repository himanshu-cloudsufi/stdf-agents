## Key Pattern (generalizable)

For Stellar technologies with zero marginal cost (AL/AI specifically):
- Infrastructure readiness comes fast — cloud delivery eliminates physical buildout lag
- Supply chain bottleneck shifts from manufacturing to WORKFORCE SKILLS (operator training, not production capacity)
- Regulatory PARTIAL is driven by employment/labor law implications, not safety standards
- This is the inverse of physical disruptions (EV, SWB) where infrastructure is the last bottleneck; for AL, infrastructure was ready before cost parity even closed
