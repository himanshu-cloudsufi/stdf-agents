## High-reliability regional sources (Tier 3)

- **ICCT, "Zero-emission medium- and heavy-duty vehicle market in China, 2024", March 2025**
  URL: https://theicct.org/publication/ze-mhdv-market-china-2024-mar25/
  Provides: port-corridor and city-cluster level adoption data; segment breakdowns; useful for YRD, PRD, BTH inference

- **IEEFA, "Surging electric truck sales stall China's LNG trucking boom", August 2025**
  URL: https://thedriven.io/2025/08/05/sales-of-battery-electric-heavy-trucks-surge-in-china-to-nearly-one-quarter-of-market/
  Provides: H1 2025 national 22% share; 116,600 units H1; most recent national anchor

- **36Kr, "Chinese New Energy Heavy Trucks: On a Rampage in the Market", July 2025**
  URL: https://eu.36kr.com/en/p/3372621858395396
  Provides: operator cost benchmarks; Central China data; LFP pack price CNY 0.5/Wh

- **China Daily, "NEV giants fueling shift to electric heavy trucks", May 2025**
  URL: https://global.chinadaily.com.cn/a/202505/26/WS6833cc95a310a04af22c1790.html
  Provides: Sichuan Yibin deployment (300+ trucks); CATL 300-station plan in 13 core regions incl. YRD/BTH/PRD

## Infrastructure reference

- **SPIC announcement Feb 2024**: 170 HDT swap stations in Gansu, Inner Mongolia, Shanxi, Hebei
  Planned, not operational as of 2025; Western China infrastructure proxy

- **CATL 75# battery pack announcement**: compatibility with >95% of HDT models; swap station rollout on 11 trunk lines

## Sparse / missing data

- **No sub-national provincial BEV-HDT registry time series** exists in public domain as of March 2026
- Xinjiang/Gansu specific shares not separately published in any primary source
- Inner Mongolia mining electrification (Yimin mine, Huawei 2025) is separate from on-road HDT statistics
