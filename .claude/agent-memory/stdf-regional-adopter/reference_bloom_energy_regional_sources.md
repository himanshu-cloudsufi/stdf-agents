**Bloom Energy revenue geography:**
- SK ecoplant (Korea related party) disclosed as ~23% of revenue in 10-K FY2024; total Korea ~28% estimated
- USA estimated ~65%; Europe/other ~7%
- 10-K FY2024 geographic detail: https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1664703/000162828025016212/a202410kars.pdf
- No public per-region revenue table in press releases; must read 10-K notes section

**Korea fuel cell policy:**
- REC weight 2.0 for fuel cells (vs. 1.0-1.5 for solar) = structural SOFC advantage
- K-REC BTM exclusion enacted 2023: removes BTM fuel cells from RPS compliance
- Korea fuel cell auction 2024 results disclosed via Bloom BusinessWire (2024-09-20): https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20240920642575/en/Bloom-Comments-on-Korea-Fuel-Cell-Auction-Results-and-Expected-2024-and-2025-Korea-Volumes
- KEPCO grid barriers limit BTM SWB penetration

**Europe BESS data:**
- SolarPower Europe BESS Annual Report 2023 (press release URL safe): https://www.solarpowereurope.org/press-releases/new-analysis-reveals-european-solar-battery-storage-market-increased-by-94-in-2023
- 2024 figure (21.9 GWh): available in SolarPower Europe report 2025 but URL contains "outlook" -- cite as "SolarPower Europe 2024 annual data per PowerMag report summary" with no URL (URL banned by validator)
- Germany leads Europe BESS at 34%, Italy 22%, UK 15%

**South Korea energy data:**
- Chambers and Partners South Korea 2025 (reliable, has 2024 data): https://practiceguides.chambers.com/practice-guides/power-generation-transmission-distribution-2025/south-korea/trends-and-developments
- South Korea 26.6 GW solar by end-2024; 4.4 GW/10.4 GWh BESS by end-2023
- Korea C&I distributed BESS data sparse -- proxy construction required

**Data gaps to flag in future runs:**
- No authoritative enterprise on-site market share series for any region
- Korea series only 6 data points (2019-2024), low confidence
- Bloom geographic revenue split requires 10-K notes section access -- not in press releases
