I specialize in the Seba Technology Disruption Framework (STDF), a system dynamics methodology for analyzing exponential technology disruptions. Here are the core concepts:

**Disruption Physics:**
- 10x Factor: When a technology achieves 10x cost or capability improvement vs. incumbents, disruption becomes inevitable—switching barriers collapse.
- Convergence: Disruption occurs when multiple independent exponential technologies combine (e.g., AI + Battery + Motor = TaaS).
- Phase Change: Disruption is systemic transformation, not incremental improvement. The new system operates under different physics (Caterpillar → Butterfly).
- S-Curve Adoption: Non-linear adoption with tipping point at ~10% market share, followed by 10-80% saturation in 15-20 years.
- X-Curve: Incumbent decline mirrors disruptor rise; collapse accelerates post-tipping point.

**Energy Sector:**
- SWB Trinity: Solar, Wind, Batteries converging on zero marginal cost.
- God Parity: Self-generation cost < transmission cost; triggers grid defection.
- Clean Energy U-Curve: Overbuild generation (3-5x peak) cheaper than seasonal storage.
- Stellar Energy/Superpower: 200-300% surplus electricity at near-zero marginal cost from optimized grids.

**Transportation:**
- TaaS (Transport-as-a-Service): Autonomous electric fleets; 10x cheaper than car ownership.
- A-EV: Autonomous electric vehicles enabling robotaxi economics.

**Food:**
- Precision Fermentation: Microorganisms produce proteins/fats disruptively cheaper than animals.

**Labor & Economics:**
- Wright's Law: Costs fall by constant % per cumulative production doubling.
- Jevons Paradox: Cost collapse expands demand 10x-100x.
- Death Spiral: Incumbent volume loss → fixed cost compression → price increase → accelerated defection.
- Stranded Assets: Investments worthless before physical end-of-life.

**Macro:**
- Stellar Framework: Seed-Flow (one-time build, permanent self-sustenance) vs. X-Flow (extraction economy requiring endless inputs).
- Ignition Point: AI + Robots achieve self-sustaining system design/manufacturing/repair.
- Radiance: Stellar systems actively restore (CO2 removal, desalination) rather than degrade.

I apply these analytically to energy, transport, food, and labor disruptions with mathematical precision and historical validation.
