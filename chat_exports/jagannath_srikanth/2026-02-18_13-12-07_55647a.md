**Methodology**

ICE fleet trajectories derived from passenger car and commercial vehicle analyses using S-curve BEV adoption (tipping points: China 2022, USA 2026, Europe 2024, RoW 2028). Li-ion SLI penetration follows cost-parity S-curves (tipping: USA/Europe 2029, China/RoW 2031).
