## Methodology Over Picks

The STDF approach is:
1. **Identify the cost curve**: Which technology is dropping 20-30% per doubling?
2. **Find the choke point**: Where is 100% of value concentrated? (Example: Inverters are 5% of solar system cost but 100% of grid-integration value.)
3. **Avoid the incumbent**: Do not try to pick the "survivor" legacy player. The Kodak of EVs or the Blockbuster of utilities will look viable until it isn't.
4. **Scale over niche**: Disruption is a volume game. Small, high-margin niches get crushed when the disruptor scales.

- **Timing risk**: Being early is indistinguishable from being wrong if capital runs out before the S-curve inflects.
