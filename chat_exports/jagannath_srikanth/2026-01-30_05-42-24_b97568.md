Legacy/residual demand (construction, electronics, industrial) is excluded from this forecast as it represents stable, non-disruptive sectors [C10].

**Regional Dynamics**

**Methodology**

(4) Legacy residual is calculated as total historical copper minus modeled categories, then projected forward at historical residual CAGR.

**Conclusion**

Copper demand growth over the next decade is a grid generation story, not a transportation story. Transportation contributes marginally because EV copper intensity gains (3.6× per vehicle) are offset by fleet saturation and slower commercial vehicle adoption.
