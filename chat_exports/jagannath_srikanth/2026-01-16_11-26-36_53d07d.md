### Required Caveats

1. **Coverage Limitation:** This analysis covers 93.1M US jobs across 186 occupations (~60% of US workforce)

2. **Adoption Lag:** Model assumes instant adoption when AI capability is reached. In practice, corporate adoption typically lags 12-24 months behind technical capability.

3. **Job Creation:** This model forecasts displacement only. It does not account for new jobs created by AI or economic adaptation (e.g., AI orchestration engineers, prompt architects, AI ethics auditors).

4. **Binary Simplification:** Tasks are modeled as fully automatable or not. In practice, partial automation and human-AI collaboration are common.

5. **80% Reliability Threshold:** Uses autonomous execution standard. Lower reliability thresholds (e.g., 50% for assisted workflows) would show earlier automation.
