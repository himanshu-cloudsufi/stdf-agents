# Lead Demand Peak Analysis - China

The key disruption mechanism is the EV transition: Battery EVs (BEVs) use significantly smaller 12V auxiliary batteries (~10-15 kg lead) compared to ICE vehicles' primary SLI batteries (~8-12 kg lead per battery, with replacement cycles). This structural shift will fundamentally reshape lead consumption patterns.

The methodology applies stock-flow accounting across all vehicle segments (passenger cars, two-wheelers, three-wheelers, commercial vehicles) and powertrains (ICE, BEV, PHEV, NGV), tracking both the installed vehicle base and battery replacement cycles. Peak demand timing emerges from the intersection of declining ICE vehicle populations (reducing both OEM and replacement demand) and the ceiling effects of EV adoption.
