### Step 2: Installed Base Accounting (SLI Batteries)

SLI demand is calculated using stock-flow accounting:

$$
SLI_{total}(t) = SLI_{OEM}(t) + SLI_{replacement}(t)
$$

$$
SLI_{OEM}(t) = \sum_{v,p} Sales_{v,p}(t) \times k_{v,p}
$$

$$
SLI_{replacement}(t) = \sum_{v,p} \frac{IB_{v,p}(t)}{\tau_{battery}} \times k_{v,p}
$$

Where:
- \( v \) = vehicle type (cars, 2W, 3W, CV)
- \( p \) = powertrain (ICE, BEV, PHEV, EV, NGV)
- \( k_{v,p} \) = lead content coefficient (kg per vehicle)
- \( \tau_{battery} \) = SLI battery lifetime (4.5 years)
- \( IB_{v,p}(t) \) = installed base (millions of units)

| Three-Wheelers | 7.0 kg | 0 kg | -- |
| Commercial Vehicles | 22.0 kg | 0 kg | 22.0 kg |

**Critical**: BEVs, PHEVs, and EVs use **lithium-ion 12V auxiliary batteries**, contributing **zero lead demand**.

$$
