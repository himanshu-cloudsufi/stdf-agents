## Lead Demand Decline Analysis: Methodology

### Step 1: Baseline Establishment

**2023 Reference Year:**
- Total global lead demand: 9,482 kt
- 10% decline threshold: 9,482 × 0.90 = 8,534 kt

### Step 2: Bottom-Up Installed Base Accounting

**Stock-Flow Equation:**
$$
IB_{v,p}(t+1) = IB_{v,p}(t) + Sales_{v,p}(t) - Scrappage_{v,p}(t)
$$

Where:
- \( v \) = vehicle type (cars, 2W, 3W, CV)
- \( p \) = powertrain (ICE, BEV, PHEV, EV, NGV)
- \( Scrappage(t) = IB(t) / Life_{asset} \)

**Lead Demand Calculation:**

**OEM Demand (New Sales):**
$$
D_{OEM}(t) = \sum_{v,p} Sales_{v,p}(t) \times k_{v,p}
$$

## Converging Technologies Driving Lead Demand Disruption

### Key Converging Technologies

**1. Lithium-Ion Battery Cost Curves**
- **2020:** $150/kWh (average pack cost)
- **2024:** $80-90/kWh (approaching TCO parity across applications)
- **2032:** $50-60/kWh (dominant economics in all battery segments)
- **Driver:** Wright's Law learning rate ~18% (cost declines 18% per doubling of cumulative production)

**2. Electric Vehicle Powertrains**
- **Cost Parity:** Passenger EVs reach cost-per-mile parity 2020-2024 (China first, USA/Europe following)
- **12V Auxiliary Systems:** BEVs use lithium-ion 12V systems, eliminating lead-acid SLI batteries entirely
- **Capability Advantage:** Instant torque, lower maintenance, software updates

**3. Industrial Battery Applications**
- **Datacenter UPS:** Li-ion 12-year life vs VRLA 5-year life = TCO parity at ~$100/kWh
- **Forklift Motive Power:** Fast charging, no acid handling, longer runtime
- **Stationary Backup:** Higher energy density, lower footprint, reduced maintenance

**4. Autonomous/FSD Technology**
- **Fleet Utilization:** Higher vehicle utilization rates in TaaS models
- **Electric Preference:** Autonomous platforms favor EV architectures (simpler, more reliable)
- **Marginal Impact on Lead:** Doesn't extend battery life or change fundamental powertrain choices

### Disruption Convergence Points

**Industrial Sector (2022-2026):**
- Li-ion pack costs cross $100/kWh threshold → TCO parity in datacenter UPS
- S-curve adoption accelerates 2024-2030 as operators realize lifetime cost savings
- Lead-acid industrial demand collapses -29.5% by 2032

**Automotive Sector (2020-2028):**
- EV cost parity in China (2020-2022), Europe (2024-2026), USA (2025-2027)
- 50%+ EV market share in China by 2025, Europe/USA reaching 40-50% by 2030
- SLI battery demand declines -7.8% by 2032 (replacement demand from aging ICE fleet moderates decline)

**Convergence Accelerant:**
- Gigafactory buildout → Learning curve acceleration → Faster cost declines → Earlier tipping points
- Regulatory support (emissions standards, ICE phase-outs) → Accelerated EV adoption → Lower Li-ion costs
- Self-reinforcing cycle: More EVs → Cheaper batteries → More industrial adoption → Further scale → Even cheaper batteries
