### 5. Forecast Methodology

The script (`scripts/forecast.py`) executed:

**a) Tipping point detection:**
- Analyzed historical cost curves
- Detected when EV TCO ≤ 70% ICE
- Results: 2W (2019), Cars (2022), 3W (2026), CV (2027)

**b) S-curve adoption modeling:**
$$\text{share}(t) = \frac{L}{1 + e^{-k(t - t_0)}}$$

**c) Installed base tracking:**
$$IB(t+1) = IB(t) + Sales(t) - Scrappage(t)$$

**d) Lead demand calculation:**
- OEM: \(Sales(t) \times Lead\_Content\)
- Replacement: \(IB(t) / Battery\_Lifetime \times Lead\_Content\)

**e) Validation:**
- Cars OEM: -3.3% variance (excellent)
- Cars replacement: +0.6% variance (excellent)
