This analysis applies disruption framework principles to lead demand forecasting, treating automotive electrification as the primary disruptive force. Lead-acid batteries face displacement from two converging disruptions: (1) Direct elimination via BEV adoption (zero lead content in electric powertrains), and (2) Substitution via lithium-ion 12V auxiliary batteries in remaining ICE fleet driven by cost parity.
