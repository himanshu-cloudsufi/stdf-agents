- **Factory automation and robotics**: Precision wiring and control systems
- **Manufacturing equipment**: Processing, assembly, material handling

---

## **5. Electronics & Consumer Products**

- **Result**: Copper demand from EVs and renewables grows **faster than GDP**, while demand from incumbent sectors (ICE, fossil generation) collapses
