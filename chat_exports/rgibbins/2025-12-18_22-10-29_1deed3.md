The core economic comparison uses LCOS ($/MWh discharged):

$$
LCOS = \frac{CAPEX_{kWh}}{Lifetime_{years} \times Cycles_{year} \times Duration_{hours} \times Efficiency} + \frac{OPEX + Fuel}{Cycles_{year} \times Duration_{hours}}
$$

### Battery Storage (4-hour, 2030)

**Capital Component:**
$$
LCOS_{capital} = \frac{163 \text{ USD/kWh}}{12 \text{ years} \times 250 \text{ cycles/yr} \times 4 \text{ hours} \times 0.88} = 15.4 \text{ USD/MWh}
$$

**Operating Component:**
$$
LCOS_{opex} = \frac{163 \times 0.02}{250 \times 4} = 3.2 \text{ USD/MWh}
$$

**Total Battery LCOS:** $18.6/MWh
