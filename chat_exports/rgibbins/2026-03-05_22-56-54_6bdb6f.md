- Displaced tasks are complex and high-skill (financial analysis, research)

- **Skill mismatch:** Displaced financial analysts cannot easily transition to executive strategy roles

**Implication:** As AI capability increases, the **pool of redeployment opportunities shrinks**, forcing a higher percentage of displaced workers into unemployment.
