## Methodology

4. **Adoption Schedule**: S-curve penetration from 5% (2025) to 20% (2027) post-tipping, with 50/50 replacement/productivity split and 80% replacement ceiling (human-in-loop minimum).

**Conversion to Unemployment Rate:**

The skill outputs FTE displacement, not unemployment. To derive unemployment rate:

1. **Scale to Full Workforce**: 18.0M FTE displaced ÷ 60% coverage = 30.0M FTE displaced economy-wide
2. **Displacement-to-Unemployment Conversion**: Apply 50% conversion factor (moderate scenario: half of displaced workers become unemployed; half transition to new roles or exit labor force)

4. **Total Unemployed**: 7.2M (baseline, BLS Jan 2026 [W1][W2]) + 15.0M = 22.2M
5. **Unemployment Rate**: 22.2M ÷ 168.5M labor force [W1][W2] = **13.2%**
