### Job Displacement Calculation

For each job category \( j \), automation percentage calculated as:

$$
A_j = \frac{\sum_{i=1}^{n} w_i \cdot a_i(C)}{\sum_{i=1}^{n} w_i}
$$

Where:
- \( A_j \) = Automation percentage for job \( j \)
- \( w_i \) = Weight for task \( i \) = \( \frac{relevance_i}{100} \times \frac{importance_i}{100} \)
- \( a_i(C) \) = Binary automation flag = 1 if \( C \geq duration_i \), else 0
- \( C \) = Current AI capability (seconds)
- \( n \) = Number of tasks for job \( j \)

**Jobs displaced:**

$$
D_j(t) = N_j \cdot A_j(C(t))
$$

Where:
- \( D_j(t) \) = Jobs displaced in category \( j \) at year \( t \)
- \( N_j \) = Total employment in category \( j \) (2024 baseline)

**Total displacement:**

$$
D_{total}(t) = \sum_{j=1}^{186} D_j(t)
$$
