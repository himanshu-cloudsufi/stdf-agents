## COST PARITY AND TIPPING POINT ANALYSIS

### **AI Cost vs. Human Cost Calculation**

For each cognitive subtask, tipping point occurs when:

$$
\text{AI Cost per Iteration} < 0.50 \times \text{Human Cost per Iteration}
$$

Where:

$$
\text{AI Cost} = \frac{\text{Cost per Million Tokens} \times \text{Tokens Required}}{1,000,000}
$$

$$
\text{Human Cost} = \text{Time per Iteration (hours)} \times \text{Hourly Pay (USD)}
$$

## **Key Risks to Thesis**

1. **Regulatory Backlash** (30% probability): Governments mandate human labor quotas or ban AI-generated content. Would delay disruption 3-5 years.

2. **Copyright Gridlock** (30% probability): Courts rule AI training violates copyright, halting model development. Would create 2-year pause in capability growth.

3. **Consumer Rejection** (25% probability): Audiences demand "human-only" content certification, creating premium pricing tier. Would bifurcate market but not stop disruption.

4. **AI Capability Plateau** (15% probability): Models fail to reach 24+ hour task duration by 2028. Would slow replacement from 38% to 25% by 2035.

5. **Energy Constraints** (10% probability): Power grid limits prevent AI compute scaling. Would increase AI costs 2-3×, delaying tipping points.

**Net Assessment**: 70% probability this transformation occurs within stated timeframes (±2 years).

## **Strategic Takeaways**

1. **The value is shifting from content production to distribution platforms with AI infrastructure.** Netflix, YouTube, Amazon win because they control full stack (production + distribution).

2. **NVIDIA captures outsized value** as every participant needs 100× more compute. This is the safest "picks-and-shovels" play.

3. **Legacy studios face "Osborne Effect"**: Announcing AI adoption signals job cuts, triggering talent exodus, accelerating decline. But NOT adopting AI guarantees cost disadvantage.

4. **The mid-tier disappears**: AI enables $100K indie films to compete with $50M studio productions. Market bifurcates into AI-native hits (low cost) and tentpole franchises (human premium). Everything in between gets squeezed.

5. **Labor displacement is front-loaded (2025-2030)**: 80% of the 38% job reduction happens in the next 5 years as tipping points cascade. Retraining window is closing.
