## Why the Model Assumes Instantaneous Implementation

The bottom-up forecasting model uses **instantaneous implementation as a theoretical upper bound** for several methodological reasons:

1. **Capability Benchmark**: It measures when AI *can* displace jobs, not when it *will*. This establishes the technological frontier.
