### 2. Cost Parity Analysis

**Human Cost (Linear Growth):**
$$
C_{human}(t) = C_{h,0} \times (1 + r_{wage})^t
$$

Where:
- \( C_{h,0} = \$30.41 \)/hr (Financial Clerks fully loaded, 2025)
- \( r_{wage} = 0.03 \) (3\% annual wage inflation)

**AI Cost (Exponential Decline):**
$$
C_{AI}(t) = C_{AI,0} \times (1 - r_{decline})^t
$$

Where:
- \( C_{AI,0} = \$5 \)/transaction (2025)
- \( r_{decline} = 0.35 \) (35\% annual cost reduction)

**Cost Ratio:**
$$
R_{cost}(t) = \frac{C_{AI}(t)}{C_{human}(t)}
$$

### 3. Automation Threshold Model

**Task Automation Condition:**
$$
\text{Automatable}(task, t) = \begin{cases}
1 & \text{if } \text{AI\_Capability}(t) \geq \text{Task\_Duration} \\
0 & \text{otherwise}
\end{cases}
$$

**Job-Level Automation Percentage:**
$$
A_{job}(t) = \frac{\sum_{i=1}^{n} w_i \times \text{Automatable}(task_i, t)}{\sum_{i=1}^{n} w_i} \times 100\%
$$

Where:
- \( w_i = \frac{relevance_i}{100} \times \frac{importance_i}{100} \)
- \( n \) = number of tasks for job category

**Example: Financial Clerks**

| Year | AI Capability | Tasks Automatable | Weighted Automation \% |
|------|--------------|-------------------|----------------------|
| 2025 | 26 min | 23\% of tasks | 43.1\% |
| 2026 | 1.4 hrs | 68\% of tasks | 83.1\% |
| 2027 | 4.8 hrs | 94\% of tasks | 99.5\% |
| 2028 | 16 hrs | 100\% of tasks | 100.0\% |

- 99.5%+ accuracy

**Operations Use Cases:**
- Process broker confirmations (PDF → structured data)
