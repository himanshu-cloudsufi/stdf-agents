**Timing and Magnitude**

Peak occurs in 2024 [C1] because:
- Passenger car EV tipping point reached in 2022 [C4] (TCO parity achieved)
- Commercial vehicle EV tipping point reached in 2024 [C5]
- ICE fleet reaches maximum before S-curve adoption accelerates replacement

**Disruption Dynamics**

China BEV share rises from 29% (2024) to 73% (2030) [C8] and 94% (2040) [C9]. This S-curve adoption trajectory drives ICE fleet replacement and lead demand collapse. The 2024-2030 decline rate of 13% [C6] accelerates post-2030 as the ICE fleet approaches zero.

- SLI passenger cars: 740 kt (2024) → 531 kt (2030) → 18 kt (2040)
- SLI commercial vehicles: 355 kt (2024) → 318 kt (2030) → 14 kt (2040)
- EV 12V auxiliary batteries grow to 260 kt (2030) but insufficient to offset ICE decline
