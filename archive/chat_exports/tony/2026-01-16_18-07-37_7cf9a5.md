4. Displacement timing: Year when job automation % crosses critical thresholds
5. Unemployment contribution: Displaced workers minus job creation (requires assumptions)

- Instant adoption when capability is reached (actual lag: 12-24 months)

- No new job creation modeled (AI creates new roles not captured in historical data)
