## God Parity Definition (STDF Framework)

**God Parity** occurs when:

**Cost of Self-Generation < Cost of Transmission & Distribution ALONE**

This is NOT a comparison to retail electricity price. Retail electricity price includes:
- Generation cost
- Transmission & Distribution (T&D) cost
- Retail margin
- Network charges

God Parity compares self-generation cost to ONLY the T&D component. At God Parity, even if grid electricity were generated for FREE, customers would still choose solar because the cost to deliver that free electricity exceeds the cost of generating it themselves. This triggers mass grid defection and the utility death spiral.
