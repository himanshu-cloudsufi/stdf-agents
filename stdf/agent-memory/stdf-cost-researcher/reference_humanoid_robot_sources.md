## Humanoid Robot Cost Data Sources by Era

### 2000–2015 (R&D era — no commercial prices)
- ASIMO ~$2.5M: widely cited industry estimate, not Honda official. Use as order-of-magnitude anchor only.
- Atlas hydraulic ~$2M–$3M: BeezBot/BotInfo industry analysis; Boston Dynamics never published a price.
- Source caveat: NO T1 sources exist for this era — all are T3 estimates.

### 2020 (First commercial humanoid)
- Agility Robotics Digit: $250,000 — announced October 2020
- Source: TechXplore news release + TechCrunch funding announcement (T3 confirmed)
- URL: https://techcrunch.com/2020/10/15/bipedal-robot-developer-agility-announces-20m-raise/

### 2023–2024 (Chinese entrants break price floor)
- Unitree H1: $90,000 — announced 2023; confirmed by New Atlas and The Robot Report
- Unitree G1: $16,000 — ICRA 2024; confirmed by The Robot Report (most reliable source)
  - URL: https://www.therobotreport.com/unitree-robotics-unveils-g1-humanoid-for-16k/
- Goldman Sachs Feb 2024 report: market-wide manufacturing cost $50k–$250k (2023) to $30k–$150k (2024)
  - URL: https://www.goldmansachs.com/pdfs/insights/pages/gs-research/global-automation-humanoid-robot-the-ai-accelerant/report.pdf

### 2025
- Unitree R1: $5,900 (39,999 CNY) — South China Morning Post
  - URL: https://www.scmp.com/tech/tech-trends/article/3319637/chinas-unitree-debuts-us5900-humanoid-robot-race-make-cheaper-products

### Maintenance cost (no historical series exists)
- Goldman Sachs 2024 estimate: $20,000–$40,000/year for commercial deployments
- Uptime: 200–500 operating hours between major service (vs. 50,000+ for industrial arms)
