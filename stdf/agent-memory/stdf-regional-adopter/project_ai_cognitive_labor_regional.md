## Key findings (pipeline run: ai-cognitive-labor, 2026-03-25)

USA leads in this disruption (not China) -- critical deviation from standard STDF pattern.

**2025 Substitution Shares (observed proxy):**
- USA: 12.0%, phase: tipping, YoY +5.0pp
- China: 8.0%, phase: tipping, YoY +3.4pp
- India: 5.5%, phase: tipping, YoY +2.4pp
- EU: 2.0%, phase: rupture, YoY +0.8pp
- RoW: 2.5%, phase: rupture, YoY +1.1pp

**Regional S-curve fits (L=88 fixed, all model-derived):**
- USA: k=0.6009, x0=2028.06, R²=0.9959 (6 data points 2020-2025)
- China: k=0.5814, x0=2028.93, R²=0.9923 (6 data points 2020-2025)
- EU: k=0.4783, x0=2032.82, R²=0.9736 (5 data points 2021-2025, Eurostat-anchored)
- India: x0=2029.59 (derived, no fit -- single anchor only)
- RoW: x0=2030.98 (derived, no fit -- heterogeneous)

**Year-behind-leader (USA):**
- China: 0.9 years behind
- India: 1.5 years behind
- RoW: 2.9 years behind
- EU: 4.8 years behind

**80% displacement years:**
- USA: 2030.4, China: 2031.3, India: 2031.9, RoW: 2033.3, EU: 2035.7

**Critical data point:** EU x0=2032.82 vs USA x0=2028.06 = 4.76-year lag. EU k=0.4783 is materially slower than global k=0.5903 -- regulatory drag is structurally reducing adoption velocity, not just delaying it.

**Why USA leads (not China):**
1. Private AI investment: USA $109B vs China $9.3B (2024, Stanford AI Index)
2. First-mover: ChatGPT/Copilot launched in USA market first
3. Highest white-collar knowledge-work concentration per worker
4. Pro-deployment regulatory stance (EO 14179/14365)
5. Stellar technology -- no manufacturing scale advantage for China; no physical buildout

**Why:** AI is a Stellar technology with no infrastructure buildout, so China's manufacturing scale and industrial policy advantages (that make it lead in physical tech disruptions) do not apply. The disruption is gated by enterprise workflow re-engineering velocity and model quality, where USA leads.

**How to apply:** For any AI/AL cognitive labor analysis, always set USA as leader (not China). Flag this deviation explicitly in output.
