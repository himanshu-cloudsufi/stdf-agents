China BEV-HDT regulatory environment as of 2024–2025:

- **Safety standard:** GB7258-2017 covers all BEV vehicles including heavy trucks
- **Emission standard:** China VI (equivalent to Euro 6) — fully in force for new HDTs, raises incumbent cost floor
- **China 7 standard:** draft stage, tightening ICE further; publication anticipated 2025, implementation before 2030
- **Purchase tax exemption:** Up to CNY 30,000 per NEV through Dec 2025; halved (CNY 15,000 cap) for 2026–2027
- **Purchase tax as % of BEV tractor price:** ~7.3% (minor, not primary driver)
- **NEV commercial vehicle target:** 15% by 2025, 30% by 2030 — already exceeded (22% in H1 2025)
- **Public-domain electrification pilot:** 2023–2025, covers logistics/port/airport fleets in 20+ cities
- **Toll exemptions:** Provincial level (Henan, Jan–Dec 2025); no national standard for heavy trucks yet
- **Mandatory ZE HDT quota:** NOT enacted; aspirational targets only
- **Swap standard:** CATL 75# is de-facto standard (95%+ compatible), no formal GB standard yet

**Key insight:** Market-driven disruption has outpaced the regulatory timetable. The 22% actual market share exceeds the 15% 2025 aspirational target, making formal mandates academic. Regulatory risk is friction-reducing, not the causal driver.

**How to apply:** Frame China regulatory environment as READY for BEV HDT. The absence of formal mandates is not a barrier because TCO parity already drives adoption. Note that incentives are step-down phased (2026–2027), which is market-appropriate.
